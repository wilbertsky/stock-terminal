What's included

Intrinsic Value (DCF)

Projects EPS 10 years forward, discounts at 15% required return. Shows margin of safety price at 50% discount.

Piotroski F-Score

Nine binary signals across profitability, leverage, and efficiency. Scores 7–9 indicate financial strength.

Quality Score

Composite 0–100 from gross margin, return on equity, and debt-to-equity. Identifies durable competitive advantages.

Momentum vs S&P 500

3, 6, and 12-month price returns benchmarked against SPY. Score starts at 50 (neutral) and shifts with outperformance.

Graham Number

√(22.5 × EPS × BVPS) — Benjamin Graham's conservative intrinsic value estimate from earnings and book value.

PEG Ratio

Peter Lynch's P/E adjusted for growth. Below 1.0 may indicate undervaluation relative to growth prospects.

Dividend Analysis

Yield, payout ratio, growth rate, and a sustainability flag. Relevant for income-focused portfolios.

Sector Screener

Ranks the top large-cap stocks within a sector using a weighted scoring model adapted to the sector's characteristics.
